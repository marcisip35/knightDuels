Knight Duels is a turned based solo game where the point of the game is to get the enemy knight to 0 health and complete all 9 levels

1. Click Play and Select A Level, there are 9 different enemy knights on each level.

2. You and your enemy will take turns playin a move

There are 2 moves available, attack and defend

Swords can swing, thrust and parry while axe's can only swing and parry

Shields block greater damage but decrease your next attack

3. You can select a Knight in the Knights tab in the Main Menu

There are 4 playable Knights, to unlock a Knight you must have that level playable

4. You can select a Weapon in the Weapons tab in the Main Menu

There are 4 Weapons available and 1 shield, you can always carry a Shield
